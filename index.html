Visualizing the bridges in Philadelphia County by their NBI rating.

Visit the map here.

This map visualizes all the bridges in Philadelphia County and their NBI rating as of 2018. This data set uses an abbreviated version of the NBI rating:
0 for not deficient and structurally sound
1 for structurally deficient in some way
2 for not deficient and structurally sound but obsolete in that it carries more traffic than it was originally intended for
This map also includes traffic data for the major arterial roads in Philadelphia and are color coded to represent how much traffic each road carries.
